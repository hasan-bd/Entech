HYTORC-Industrial Bolting Solutions

We, Entech Corporation, introduce ourselves as a complete bolting solution provider being only authorised partner and national distributor of HYTORC-USA in the Bangladesh market. Our vision is to be the pioneer in establishing bolting services as an industry and believe in providing end to end solutions from tool supply to project management. Being the oldest and largest torque tool manufacturer, HYTORC is the most trusted name in the industry. From mining equipment to refineries, wind turbines, power plants, and steel mills; HYTORC industrial bolting systems can be customised for every bolting application imaginable.

In collaboration with Advanced Bolting Solutions Pvt Ltd (ABS), we are actively involved in professional training, AMC’s, tool rentals, bolting contracts, customized solutions and handling complete projects for major private and government corporations in Bangladesh in sectors ranging from steel, cement, refinery, railway, wind, power, mining etc. With over two decades of experience in making complex industrial bolting jobs safer and simpler, Advanced Bolting Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a leading multinational company with international presence in India and UAE that offer precision bolting solutions through consultative sales of HYTORC torqueing tools.


As a customer-centric brand, Entech is committed to deliver joint-integrity solutions. To achieve their vision, HYTORC offers a wide range of torque wrenches powered under multiple sources such as hydraulic, pneumatic, and electric range. These tools are consistently developed to integrate new innovations to further widen their scope of products and services.

In Partnership at exclusive basis, Entech Corporation provide products and services of HYTORC and ABS in Bangladesh.
